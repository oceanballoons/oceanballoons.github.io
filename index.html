    WE ARE THE SOLUTION

    With decades of experience in software development and database design, we understand the importance of communication with executive teams, stakeholders, and amongst teams and are experienced in Agile, Scrum, DevOps, and Six Sigma best practices. Our team is experienced in building and operating systems, CI/CD automation, monitoring and metrics gathering, strategic and financial planning, and domain knowledge in Video services, User Interface design, Web Services, and Database development. We are confident that our team can provide a comprehensive solution to the Alberta Land Titles Registry System, ensuring a smooth transition, minimal customer impact, and an efficient and reliable system.
